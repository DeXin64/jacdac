# LED status

JACDAC supports a status LED to convey various status of the module and protocols.

## Startup

lights at 100% for 270ms, then 5.9% for rest of 530ms, 
then goes into application that turns it on full for 200ms

<StatusLEDAnimation status="startup" />

## Connected

Synchronized fast blink 50us every 500ms

<StatusLEDAnimation status="connected" />

## Identify

50ms every 150ms (50 on, 100 off) seven times (i.e. for 1 second)

<StatusLEDAnimation status="identify" />

## Disconnected 

5ms every 250ms

<StatusLEDAnimation status="disconnected" />

## Panic

fast blink 70ms on, 70ms off - 30 times (4.2 seconds) before a reboot

<StatusLEDAnimation status="panic" />

## Bootloader

every 524ms it changes from 5.9% and 1.6% (i.e. 1 sec duty cycle)

<StatusLEDAnimation status="bootloader" />
